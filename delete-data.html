Account Deletion Request Confirmation

We understand that you would like to delete your account and all associated data from our platform. Please note the following:

  1. What happens next?
    • Your account and all associated data (including personal information, purchase history, and preferences) will be permanently deleted.
    • This process is irreversible, and once completed, we cannot restore your account or data.
  2. How long will it take?
    • Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
  3. Exceptions:
    • Some data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or security purposes, such as transaction records, but it will no longer be associated with your account.
  4. Need assistance?

    If you have questions or encounter any issues, please contact our support team at emporiumsocialcommerce@gmail.com.

We’re sorry to see you go, and we thank you for being part of our platform.
